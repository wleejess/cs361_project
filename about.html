your clean & simple, task management system.

Our objective is to support you and the things you set out to do.
There are many explanations for why we either 1) don't complete a task, or 2) don't complete a task on time.


"To Be Determined" helps you with:

  • Organization: Providing a structured way for you to track and view about your tasks
  • Prioritization: As you add items to your tracker, the list will refresh and be ordered by due dates.
  • Time Management: By seeing how many tasks you have & when they're do, you can estimate how much time to allocate for each task.
  • Efficiency: Streamline your workflow so that you can get *one thing* done at a time, instead of getting *part* of *everything* done.

Tips & Tricks

  • Write down everything you need to do, no matter how simple/complicated it may seem.
  • Clarify each task by being specific.
  • Evaluate your current status.
    What *can* you do at the moment? Does the task require a laptop? To travel somewhere?
  • Organize your tasks.
  • Start somewhere.
    The most difficult part is figuring out where to start. Break things down and find the path of least resistance.
